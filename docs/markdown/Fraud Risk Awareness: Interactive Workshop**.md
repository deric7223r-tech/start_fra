Fraud Risk Awareness: Interactive Workshop**
### Overview:
This web app provides a comprehensive, user-friendly experience for your 30-
minute Fraud Risk Awareness Workshop for trustees, leadership, and budget-
holders. The goal is to raise awareness of fraud risks and compliance 
requirements, with a focus on regulatory updates and practical next steps.
---
### Key Features:
1. **Interactive Briefing Slides**:
   * Users can navigate through slides that cover critical topics, including the 
regulatory landscape, fraud types, defense strategies, and organisational 
impact.
   * Each slide will feature a "Discussion Prompt" or a "Quick Quiz" to reinforce 
key points and engage users.
2. **Real-Time Polling & Feedback**:
   * Conduct live polls with attendees to gauge understanding and capture input 
on fraud risks within their organisation.
   * Attendees can submit questions via a Q&A section, with the option to 
upvote questions from others.
3. **Downloadable Resources**:
   * A dedicated section for downloading materials such as:
     * Fraud Risk Self-Assessment Checklist
     * Budget-Holder Guidance Document
     * 90-Day Implementation Roadmap
   * Option to send these resources via email for easy access after the 
workshop.
4. **Case Studies & Scenario-Based Exercises**:
   * Provide realistic case studies tailored to the specific sector (Public, Charity, 
or Private) for deeper understanding.
   * Interactive fraud scenarios where users can make decisions and see the 
outcomes of different actions, fostering practical application of the learned 
concepts.

5. **Progress Tracker**:
   * Attendees can track their progress through the workshop, marking sections 
as complete and receiving recommendations for next steps based on their 
responses.
   * After the workshop, they can view a personal action plan based on their 
organisation's needs and committed actions.
6. **Customisable Content**:
   * Depending on the sector (public, charity, private), users can choose specific 
case studies, examples, and frameworks relevant to their industry, ensuring the 
content remains sector-specific and highly relevant.
7. **Multi-Device Compatibility**:
   * The app is optimized for desktops, tablets, and smartphones, ensuring 
seamless access to materials and interactivity no matter the device.
8. **Certification & Completion Badge**:
   * Once the workshop is completed, users will receive a certificate of 
completion with a summary of their action steps, helping them stay on track 
with fraud risk management.
---
### User Flow:
1. **Welcome Screen**:
   * Introduce the workshop's objectives, duration, and audience.
   * Option to start or continue the session.
2. **Pre-Workshop Materials**:
   * Download or read materials before starting the session.
   * Brief overview of the regulatory landscape and fraud risks.
3. **Interactive Briefing**:
   * Users can navigate through the sections of the workshop.
   * For each section (e.g., "Regulatory Landscape", "Organisational Impact"), 
there will be:
     * A brief, informative slide

     * Discussion prompts or polls
     * A sector-specific case study or scenario
4. **Practical Next Steps**:
   * Based on the user's responses during the workshop, an action plan is 
generated for the organisation to start the fraud risk assessment process.
5. **Wrap-Up**:
   * A summary of key points learned, the next steps for the organisation, and 
downloadable resources for reference.
   * A final survey to gather feedback and gauge the effectiveness of the 
workshop.
---
### Design Approach:
* **Simplicity**: Use clean, modern visuals with intuitive navigation. Avoid 
overwhelming users with too many options on any single page.
* **Sophistication**: Use professional colors, fonts, and layout, incorporating 
the branding of your organisation for a polished, corporate feel.
* **Interactivity**: Ensure that the app is engaging with real-time inputs from 
users (polls, scenario-based questions, etc.).
