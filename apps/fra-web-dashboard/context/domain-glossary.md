# Risk Aware Domain Glossary

## Regulatory Terms

**ECCTA 2023**
Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023 - UK legislation introducing corporate liability for fraud.

**Failure to Prevent Fraud**
New offence under ECCTA where organisations can be prosecuted if an employee commits fraud for the organisation's benefit, unless "reasonable procedures" were in place.

**Reasonable Procedures**
Defence against failure to prevent fraud - documented policies, controls, training, and monitoring that demonstrate proactive fraud prevention.

## Fraud Types

**Internal Fraud**
Fraud committed by employees, trustees, or volunteers within the organisation.

**External Fraud**
Fraud committed by third parties against the organisation.

**Asset Misappropriation**
Theft or misuse of organisational assets (cash, inventory, equipment).

**Expense Fraud**
False or inflated expense claims for personal gain.

**Payroll Fraud**
Ghost employees, timesheet manipulation, or unauthorized salary changes.

**Procurement Fraud**
Bid rigging, kickbacks, or conflicts of interest in purchasing.

**Invoice Fraud**
False invoices or redirected payments to fraudulent accounts.

**CEO Fraud / BEC**
Business Email Compromise - impersonation of senior executives to authorize fraudulent payments.

**Vendor Impersonation**
Fraudsters posing as legitimate suppliers to redirect payments.

## Control Terms

**Segregation of Duties (SoD)**
Dividing responsibilities so no single person controls an entire transaction.

**Dual Authorisation**
Requiring two people to approve high-value or high-risk transactions.

**Whistleblowing**
Confidential reporting of suspected wrongdoing.

**Continuous Monitoring**
Automated detection of anomalies in financial transactions.

## Platform Terms

**Facilitator**
User who leads a workshop session for a group of participants.

**Participant**
User who attends and completes the workshop.

**Session Code**
Unique code to join a live facilitated workshop session.

**Action Plan**
Personal commitments made by participants after completing the workshop.

**Certificate**
Document issued upon workshop completion for compliance evidence.
