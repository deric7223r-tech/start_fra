## Review Criteria

### Regulatory Accuracy
- [ ] References current UK legislation
- [ ] Cites correct act names and years
- [ ] Reflects latest guidance from regulators

### Tone & Accessibility
- [ ] Professional but approachable
- [ ] Appropriate for senior stakeholders
- [ ] Free of jargon or jargon is explained
- [ ] Reading level appropriate for audience

### British English
- [ ] UK spelling (organisation, colour, defence)
- [ ] UK date format references (DD/MM/YYYY)
- [ ] UK-specific terminology

### Accuracy
- [ ] Statistics are current and sourced
- [ ] Examples are realistic and relevant
- [ ] Case studies reflect actual fraud patterns

## Sources to Verify Against

- Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023
- Public Sector Fraud Authority publications
- National Audit Office reports
- Cabinet Office Counter Fraud Standards
